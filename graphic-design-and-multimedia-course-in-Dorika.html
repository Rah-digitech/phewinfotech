GRAPHIC DESIGN & MULTIMEDIA PROFESSIONALS (GDMP)

Best Graphic Design & Multimedia Training in Delhi



rahdigitechacademy offers professional training course for Graphic Designing. Graphic Designing certificate program (6 months) introduces students to Graphic Design Concepts, Basic Design Principles and Industry Best Design Softwares. Graphic Design certificate can enhance your ability to "Get Your Dream Job", higher pay scale. Our certification training program will simply help you do work faster and more efficiently by developing your design and software proficiency.

Graphic Design program at rahdigitechacademy is designed for individuals who are interested in applying their creativity to communicating ideas and concepts in both print and digital media.

In short, Graphic Design is the process of using the computer and specific types of software to combine ideas and artworks to produce documents properly formatted for print, web, or mobile devices such as UI Designs, Illustrations, Digital/Matte Art, Logo Design, Packaging etc.
